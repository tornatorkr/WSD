Joe's Bio

From:

Ghent, NY

Genre:

Country, Blues, Rock

Joe has played from Plattsburgh, NY to Nashville, TN. His style is a mix of Blues with a bit of Comedy. Inspired by artists like B.B.King, Buddy Guy, Steve Ray Vaughn, Jimmy Hendrix, Allman Brothers and Ray Stevens, you never know what to expect from Joe.
